Termite Problems in Surat – Act Before It's Too Late

Surat's warm and humid climate creates ideal conditions for termites to thrive. These silent destroyers can eat through wooden furniture, flooring, and even concrete foundations without being noticed for years. A small colony can cause lakhs in structural damage. Don't wait until you see visible signs – professional termite control is an investment in your property's future.

Signs of Termite Infestation

  • Mud tubes on walls or foundation
  • Hollow-sounding wood when tapped
  • Discarded wings near windows or doors
  • Cracked paint or bubbling walls
  • Tight-fitting doors or windows (warped frames)
  • Frass (termite droppings) looking like sawdust
  • Live termites in wood or soil
