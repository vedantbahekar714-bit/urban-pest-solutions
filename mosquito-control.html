Mosquito Problem in Surat – A Serious Health Threat

Surat's warm, humid climate and monsoon season create ideal breeding grounds for mosquitoes. They transmit deadly diseases like dengue, malaria, chikungunya, and Zika. A single stagnant water source can breed thousands of mosquitoes. Professional control is essential to protect your family.

Signs of Mosquito Infestation

  • Frequent mosquito bites – especially at dawn/dusk
  • Larvae in standing water – wriggling worms in pots, drains
  • Buzzing sound near ears at night
  • Adult mosquitoes resting on walls or ceilings
  • Mosquitoes indoors even with closed windows
  • Breeding sites – coolers, flower pots, bird baths

Frequently Asked Questions – Mosquito Control

How long does mosquito fogging last?
Fogging provides immediate adult mosquito kill and lasts 2-4 weeks depending on weather and surrounding breeding sites.
Is fogging safe for my family and pets?
Yes. We use pyrethroid-based fogging liquids that are safe for humans and pets once settled (30 minutes). We recommend staying indoors during fogging.
How much does mosquito control cost in Surat?
Single fogging for a standard home (500 sq ft) starts from ₹1,500. Larvicide treatment from ₹2,000. Seasonal packages available at discount.
Can you treat my society / apartment complex?
Absolutely. We provide bulk fogging and larvicide services for residential societies, gated communities, and commercial complexes.
How can I prevent mosquitoes myself?
Remove standing water weekly, clean gutters, use mosquito nets, and keep overhead tanks covered. But professional treatment is recommended for severe infestations.
