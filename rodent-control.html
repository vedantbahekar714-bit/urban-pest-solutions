Rodent Problem in Surat – Health & Safety Hazard

Rats and mice are common in Surat's residential and commercial areas. They spread diseases like leptospirosis, hantavirus, and salmonella, contaminate food, and gnaw on electrical wires – causing fire hazards. A single pair can produce up to 2,000 offspring in a year. Don't wait until you see droppings or hear scratching at night.

Signs of Rodent Infestation

  • Droppings – small, dark pellets near food or cabinets
  • Gnaw marks on wires, furniture, or food packets
  • Scratching sounds at night in walls or ceilings
  • Grease marks along walls from their fur
  • Nests made of shredded paper or fabric
  • Strange odour – a musky smell in closed areas
  • Live or dead rodents – especially near garbage
