Bed Bug Problem – Don't Let Them Bite

Bed bugs are tiny, nocturnal pests that hide in mattresses, furniture, and cracks. They cause itchy bites, allergic reactions, and sleepless nights. They reproduce quickly – a female lays 5-7 eggs per week. Professional treatment is the only way to eliminate them completely.

Signs of Bed Bug Infestation

  • Rusty or reddish stains on sheets or mattresses
  • Dark spots (faecal matter) on bedding
  • Eggs or eggshells – tiny pale yellow specks
  • Live bed bugs – especially in mattress seams
  • Musty odour from their scent glands
  • Itchy bites in a line or cluster on skin
  • Shed skins – pale yellow exoskeletons

Frequently Asked Questions – Bed Bug Treatment

How long does bed bug treatment take?
Heat treatment takes 4-6 hours for a standard bedroom. Steam treatment takes 2-3 hours. The room is ready for use immediately after cooling.
Is heat treatment safe for my belongings?
Yes. Professional heat treatment raises room temperature to 60°C – safe for furniture, electronics, and fabrics. Remove only heat-sensitive items like candles or medications.

Do I need to throw my mattress?
No. Professional treatment kills bed bugs inside the mattress. We recommend using a bed bug-proof encasement after treatment.

Heat treatment is usually one-time. We recommend a follow-up inspection after 14 days. Free re-treatment if any bed bugs are found.
