Cockroach Problem in Surat – Health Risk You Can't Ignore

Cockroaches thrive in Surat's warm, humid climate – especially in kitchens, drains, and commercial spaces. They spread diseases like salmonella, E. coli, and trigger asthma and allergies. A single female can produce up to 300 offspring in a year. Don't let these pests compromise your family's health or your business reputation.

Signs of Cockroach Infestation

  • Droppings like coffee grounds or black pepper
  • Musty odour – a strong, unpleasant smell
  • Egg cases (oothecae) in corners or behind appliances
  • Live or dead cockroaches – especially at night
  • Shed skins – nymphs leave behind exoskeletons
  • Smear marks on walls or floors from their bodies
  • Damaged food packaging – chew marks on boxes
