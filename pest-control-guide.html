📘 Pest Awareness Guide

Pest Control Guide for Surat Homes & Businesses

Understanding pests is the first step to protecting your property and family. This guide helps you identify, prevent, and know when to call professionals.

Why Pest Control is Important

Health Risks

Cockroaches trigger asthma, mosquitoes spread dengue & malaria, rodents carry diseases. Protect your family's health.

Property Damage

Termites silently destroy wooden structures. Rodents chew wires – fire hazard. Early prevention saves lakhs.

Hygiene & Reputation

For businesses, pest sightings ruin trust. For homes, they contaminate food and spread bacteria.

Common Pests in Surat

Termites

Silent destroyers. They feed on wood, furniture, and even paper. Thrive in Surat's humid climate.

Cockroaches

Common in kitchens and drains. Spread germs like Salmonella and E. coli.

Rodents

Rats and mice gnaw on wires, contaminate food, and reproduce quickly.

Mosquitoes

Breed in stagnant water. Vector for dengue, chikungunya, malaria.

Bed Bugs

Hide in mattresses and furniture. Cause itchy bites and sleep loss.

Early Warning Signs of Infestation

Termites

Mud tubes on walls, hollow-sounding wood, discarded wings near windows.

Cockroaches

Droppings like coffee grounds, musty odor, egg cases in corners.

Rodents

Gnaw marks, droppings, scratching sounds at night, grease marks.

Mosquitoes

Frequent bites, larvae in standing water, buzzing sound.

Bed Bugs

Rusty stains on sheets, tiny blood spots, live bugs in mattress seams.

Simple Prevention Tips

Keep Clean

Wipe counters, sweep floors, store food in sealed containers.

Remove Standing Water

Change pet water daily, clear clogged drains – stops mosquitoes.

Seal Entry Points

Caulk cracks, fix window screens, use door sweeps.

Trim Vegetation

Keep shrubs away from walls – rodents use them as bridges.

Declutter

Reduce cardboard boxes and piles where pests hide.

Regular Inspections

Check under sinks, behind appliances, and in basements.

When DIY is Not Enough – Call Professionals

If you see large numbers of pests, recurring infestations, or property damage, it's time to call experts. Professionals use safe, targeted treatments that are more effective and longer-lasting than store-bought sprays.

  • Termite colonies deep inside walls
  • Rodent nests in attic or crawl spaces
  • Bed bugs that keep coming back
  • Large cockroach or mosquito breeding sites
